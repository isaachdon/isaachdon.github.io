Ride Recyclers, at its core, is a mechanic shop. We will modify or fix your car, and that is the essential service we provide. But we decided that wasn't enough. As a result of this, we want to further our services by teaching you how to modify or fix your car while we work. When you do business with us, you have the added option of becoming a student of one of our knowledgeable coaches so you can continue work on your car on your own.

If you choose to learn with us, you will have the opportunity to better your understanding of automobiles in general, furthering the development of the car community. You could learn anything from a basic oil change, to a full rebuild of a classic muscle car. The possibilities are virtually endless!
